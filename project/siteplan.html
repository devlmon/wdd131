Overview

Site Name

Terraria Appreciation Page.

- This name is to clarify that the page is intended to show appreciation for Terraria and discuss the game in a positive manner.

Site Purpose

- The page will display relevant and interesting information about the game that could spark the reader's curiosity. This information will range from general descriptions of the game to images of its diverse gameplay and links to useful resources.

Scenarios

For the Terraria Appreciation Page...

- What does Terraria offer me compared to other games (i.e. Minecraft)?

- I feel lost in the game. Is there a guide that can help me progress?
